esafety Ltd

 

helping to safeguard children and young people online

esafety Ltd is run by Karl Hopwood, an international expert on esafety issues for schools, children and young people.

Karl is a member of the UK Council for Child Internet Safety and works with a number of key players in the UK and Europe. He provides a range of services including consultancy, staff training and lessons and workshops for pupils. Karl presents regularly at conferences in the UK and abroad and provides training for schools, colleges, universities and LSCBs. Karl works as an in-house consultant for Insafe, the coordinator of the Safer Internet Centres in the EU.
